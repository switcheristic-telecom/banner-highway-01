Banner Highway 01

Get on the fast lane, 汽车是旅行的工具 is the first installment in Banner Highway, a browser-based visual poetry series reimagining classic Burma-Shave roadside advertisement-poems for the Information Superhighway. The banners come from Banner Depot 2000, a web-based archive of 1990s–early 2000s banner ads (banner-depot-2000.net).

By Switcheristic Telecommunications : Richard Lewei Huang & Yufeng Zhao. Logo design by Kay Zeng.
